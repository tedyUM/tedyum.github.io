bio: I am a PhD candidate at the Temporal Dynamics Lab and the Brain Stimulation and Cognition lab, supervised by Sanne ten Oever, Felix Duecker and Alexander Sack. Before joining this lab, I explored how internal processes, such as mental imagery, prior knowledge, and memory, influences perception and linguistic processing (Donders Institute & Max Planck Institute). I am happy to extent this line of interest by investigating how we can keep multiple items in mind, using simultaneous fMRI-EEG-TMS. While exploring this topic, I am inspired by a strong methodological and theoretical focus, given my double background in Cognitive Neuroscience and Philosophy (Radboud University, Nijmegen).
